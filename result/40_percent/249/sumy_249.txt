Tony Blair has said he will not take part in a TV debate with his political rivals ahead of the next election.In October Tory leader Michael Howard said Mr Blair would be running scared if he refused calls to go head-to-head.Before the 2001 election, plans for a debate between Mr Blair, William Hague and Charles Kennedy collapsed.In 1997 a debate between Mr Blair and John Major was also cancelled when a format could not be agreed.
