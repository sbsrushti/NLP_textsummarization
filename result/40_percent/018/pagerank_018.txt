Foreign Secretary Jack Straw will visit Auschwitz for the 60th anniversary of the former Nazi concentration camp's liberation, it has been announced.In Britain, the Queen and Prince Philip will lead the nation's commemoration at a service in Westminster Hall, London.Shadow Foreign Secretary Michael Ancram said: "I am glad the foreign secretary has finally been shamed into representing Britain at this important act of commemoration.The Queen's grandson, Prince Harry, sparked outrage earlier this week after photographs of him wearing a Nazi uniform at a costume party emerged.Prince Edward will also join the UK delegation in Poland for National Holocaust Memorial Day on 27 January.
